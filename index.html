About Me

Transport Geography Enthusiastic

I am an Urban Planner & passionate about the intersection of transportation and geography, I am dedicated to leveraging my expertise in both fields to drive innovative solutions and foster sustainable development. With a diverse skill set and a commitment to lifelong learning, I thrive in dynamic environments where I can apply my knowledge to address complex challenges. I have completed my Bachelor of Urban and Regional Planning (URP) from Khulna University of Engineering and Technology (KUET), Bangladesh in 2023

  • Transportation Analysis: Proficient in analyzing transportation data, conducting route optimization, and assessing the socio-economic impacts of transportation policies and infrastructure projects.
  • Geospatial Technologies: Experienced in utilizing Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Remote sensing (RS), and spatial analysis techniques to visualize and interpret transportation patterns and spatial relationships.
  • Policy Advocacy: A proactive advocate for sustainable and equitable transportation policies, I engage with stakeholders, policymakers, and communities to promote initiatives that enhance mobility, accessibility, and environmental resilience.
  • Research and Writing:Skilled in conducting in-depth research, synthesizing complex information, and crafting compelling narratives through articles, reports, and presentations.
